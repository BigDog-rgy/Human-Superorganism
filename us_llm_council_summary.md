# LLM Council: Top 12 Living US Prime Movers (2026)

*Generated: February 17, 2026*

## Methodology: Three-Stage Council Process (Karpathy Method)

**Stage 1 - Independent Responses**: Four leading LLMs (Claude Opus 4.6, GPT-5.2, Grok 4.1, Gemini 3 Pro) independently generated their top 12 lists (domestic US focus).

**Stage 2 - Peer Review**: Each model anonymously reviewed and ranked the other models' responses for accuracy, reasoning quality, and comprehensiveness.

**Stage 3 - Chairman Synthesis**: Claude Opus 4.6, serving as Chairman, synthesized all responses and peer reviews into this final authoritative list.

**Consensus Notes**: Universal #1 or #2 across all four models; three of four placed him first, and peer reviews unanimously affirmed the President must top a domestic-influence list.
