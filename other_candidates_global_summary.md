> These lists surface influential global figures **not** captured by existing heads-of-state, billionaire, or top-executive tracking lists. Each category explicitly excludes current heads of state, top-tier executives, and billionaires.

## Methodology: Three-Stage Council Process (Karpathy Method)

**Stage 1 — Independent Responses**: Four leading LLMs (Claude Opus 4.6, GPT-5.2, Grok 4.1, Gemini 3 Pro) independently generated top-20 lists per category.

**Stage 2 — Peer Review**: Each model anonymously reviewed and ranked the other models' responses for accuracy, reasoning quality, and category adherence.

**Stage 3 — Chairman Synthesis**: Claude Opus 4.6, serving as Chairman, synthesized all inputs into the final authoritative lists.
